Friday October 11th 5pm - 9pm

TOYOTA AUTONATION | 6300 W SAHARA AVE LAS VEGAS NEVADA, 89146

IDOLSGANG & VEGAS TOYOTA OFFROAD IN PARTNERSHIP WITH LAS VEGAS METRO POLICE DEPARTMENT

Every year, the Las Vegas Metro Police Department throws the biggest Trunk or Treat event in the Las Vegas Valley, and it just keeps getting better! Last year, we had an incredible turnout with over 10,000 kids joining in the Halloween fun. This year, we're gearing up for another massive celebration, expecting close to 10,000 kids to dive into the spooky festivities. Its a blast for all ages, with trick-or-treating, and decked-out off-road vehicles to marvel at.


Idolsgang and Vegas Toyota Offroad are pumped to team up again, giving back to our amazing community by collecting candy and donations for this event. Our first-ever Trucks & Treats in 2023 was a smash hit! With the help of our awesome sponsors, we filled two truck beds with candy and raised over $1,000 in donations. More than 100 rigs showed up for the candy drive, and this year, were going even bigger! We cant wait to see what we can achieve together as we aim to collect even more candy and donations for the kids.
