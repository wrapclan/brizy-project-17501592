6.1.24 - GEARS & FINDS

Idolsgang & 4 The Truck presents Gears & Finds. Come join us for a fun filled night. Bring your truck parts, camping and overlanding gear to barter. Bring your family to enjoy good eats and check out the local vendors. Most importantly, let's help raise money for two outstanding non-profit organizations in SNORR (Southern Nevada Off-Road Recovery) & The Blue Ribbon Coalition.

SNORR is a volunteer-based organization that assists in recovering stranded off-road vehicles. They operate entirely on donations and often cover costs out of pocket, offering their services free of charge and saving our butts when we're stranded.


The Blue Ribbon Coalition focuses on legal advocacy to preserve off-road trails, defending against closures by organizations like the BLM. Their work is crucial in maintaining our access to these trails.

Both SNORR & The Blue Ribbon Coalition deserve a huge THANK YOU to the volunteer work they provide and we as a collective off-road community have an opportunity to give back to the organizations that consistently support us.
