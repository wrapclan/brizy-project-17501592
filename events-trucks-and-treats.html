Idolsgang presents "Trucks & Treats"

Friday, October 13th from 5:00pm - 9:00pm - ALL RIGS WELCOME!

On October 13th from 5:00pm to 9:00pm! We’re revving up our engines for a candy collection drive to sweeten up the Vegas Toyota Offroad & LVMPD Trunk or Treat event on October 26th. Be part of this delicious cause, and don’t miss your chance to win thousands of dollars in raffle prizes. Enjoy a feast from food trucks and discover fantastic offerings from local businesses. It’s a night of treats, trucks, and terrific community spirit – mark your calendar and bring your candy donations!
