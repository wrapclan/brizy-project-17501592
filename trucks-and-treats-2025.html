SATURDAY October 11th 5pm - 9pm

TOYOTA AUTONATION | 6300 W SAHARA AVE LAS VEGAS NEVADA, 89146

IDOLSGANG & VEGAS TOYOTA OFFROAD IN PARTNERSHIP WITH LAS VEGAS METRO POLICE DEPARTMENT

Every year, the Las Vegas Metro Police Department hosts the biggest Trunk or Treat event in the Las Vegas Valleyand it only gets better! In 2024, we had an incredible turnout with over 10,000 kids joining in the Halloween fun. This year, were gearing up for another huge celebration, once again expecting close to 10,000 kids to dive into the spooky festivities. Its a family-friendly blast for all ages, featuring trick-or-treating and epic off-road vehicles decked out for Halloween.

Idolsgang and Vegas Toyota Offroad are excited to team up once again to give back to our amazing community by collecting candy and donations for this event. In 2024, Trucks & Treats was a massive success! With the help of our incredible sponsors, we raised $9,000 in donations, collected three truck beds full of candy, and packed the night with unforgettable eventsincluding a flex ramp competition with championship belts, a cook-off, a DJ, a barber giving free haircuts, and over $30,000 in raffle prizes.

In 2025, were going even bigger! We cant wait to see what we can achieve together as we aim to collect even more candy and donations for the kids.
