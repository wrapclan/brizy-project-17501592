ABOUT

My name is Mike, and this page showcases my build while serving as a resource for anyone seeking inspiration for their own projects. Here, you’ll find insights into the companies I work with, the features I’ve been fortunate to be a part of, and opportunities for collaboration if you’re a brand looking to connect. Feel free to reach out with any questions—I’m passionate about off-roading and always eager to help others.


I am the president of Idolsgang, an off-road lifestyle movement dedicated to giving back to the off-road and local communities. Our mission is to unite like-minded enthusiasts and create something greater with real impact. Each year, we host two major charity events and collaborate with non-profit organizations such as Southern Nevada Offroad Recovery (SNORR), the Las Vegas Metro Police Department, the BlueRibbon Coalition, and more.


Additionally, I work with and help organize events for Vegas Toyota Offroad, the largest off-road community in the Las Vegas Valley. Sharing the same core values as Idolsgang, Vegas Toyota Offroad plays a key role in strengthening the local off-road scene.
